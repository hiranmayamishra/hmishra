Current Research Interests
Broadly my research interest is on strong interactions. In particular I am interested in hadron properties as related to the ground state of QCD. My current research interests are:

  • Transport properties of matter in extreme conditions
  • QCD phase diagram.
  • Color superconductivity.
  • Neutron star structure and properties.
  • Quarkonium properties at finite temperature and density.
